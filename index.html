Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Tajikistan is from May to October. Summer months (June-August) are perfect for mountain trekking, while spring (April-May) offers beautiful blooming landscapes.

Essential Travel Tips

Visa Information

Most visitors need an e-visa, which can be obtained online before travel.

Currency

The Tajikistani Somoni (TJS) is the local currency. ATMs are available in major cities.

Electricity

220V with European-style plugs (types C and F).

Internet

Good coverage in cities, limited in remote areas. Local SIM cards available.
